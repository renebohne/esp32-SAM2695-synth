XIAO-SYNTH Web Flasher

Flash the latest firmware for your Seeed Studio XIAO board directly from the browser. No drivers needed (in Chrome/Edge).

Instructions:
1. Plug in your XIAO board.
2. Put it into bootloader mode (Hold BOOT, press RESET, release BOOT).
3. Click the "Connect" button that appears, select the correct COM/Serial port, and click "Connect".
4. Click the "Flash" button for your board.

Connect
Your browser doesn't support Web Serial. Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
