XIAO-SYNTH Web Flasher

Flash the latest firmware for your Seeed Studio XIAO board directly from the browser.

Instructions:
1. Plug in your XIAO board.
2. Put it into bootloader mode (Hold BOOT, press RESET, release BOOT).
3. Click the button for your board. The flasher will first connect, then flash.

Flash ESP32-S3 Flash ESP32-C3 Flash ESP32-C6
Your browser doesn't support Web Serial. Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
